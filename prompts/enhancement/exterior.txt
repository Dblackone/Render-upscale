EXTERIOR ARCHITECTURAL ENHANCEMENT:

FACADE AND BUILDING ENVELOPE:
- Enhance all facade materials to architectural photography standard
- Add realistic weathering, material aging, and environmental patina appropriate to building age
- Ensure glazing systems show correct reflections of sky and surroundings
- Metal panels, cladding systems, and sunshading devices should cast realistic shadows

LANDSCAPE AND SITE:
- Add lush, climate-appropriate planted landscape around the building
- Foreground planting creates depth and frames the building naturally
- Ground plane shows realistic paving texture, joins, weathering, and material variation
- Add natural debris, fallen leaves, or seasonal character where appropriate

HUMAN ENTOURAGE:
- Add 3-8 people at correct scale to convey the building's human dimension
- People should be naturally posed: walking, standing, conversing
- Demographic variety appropriate to building type and context
- People enhance spatial legibility without dominating the composition

SKY AND ENVIRONMENT:
- Dramatic, photographic sky with dynamic clouds and atmospheric depth
- Correct color temperature for time of day
- Background urban or landscape context adds depth without competing with subject

STREET AND URBAN CONTEXT:
- Realistic road surface, kerbs, paving, and street furniture
- Parked or passing vehicles at correct scale where contextually appropriate
- Street trees and planting integrated naturally into the scene
