URBAN DESIGN AND STREETSCAPE ENHANCEMENT:

STREET LIFE AND ACTIVITY:
- Populate with a diverse, realistic crowd at correct scale
- Varied activities: walking, cycling, sitting, shopping, conversing
- Correct demographic mix for the urban context
- Street café seating, market stalls, or retail activity where appropriate
- Cyclists, scooters, and pedestrians share the space naturally

VEHICLES:
- Realistic parked and moving vehicles appropriate to the street type
- Correct scale and contemporary models
- Vehicles add urban realism without overwhelming the public space

STREET FURNITURE AND INFRASTRUCTURE:
- Street lighting, signage, bollards, and urban furniture look realistic and weathered
- Utility infrastructure integrated naturally
- Planting beds, tree grates, cycle stands, and public seating

URBAN LANDSCAPE:
- Street trees with full, realistic canopies — species appropriate to climate
- Seasonal planting in beds and containers
- Ground surface shows natural wear, weathering, and variation

BUILDINGS IN BACKGROUND:
- Background buildings provide urban density and depth
- Realistic facade materials, windows reflecting sky
- Appropriate building heights and massing for context

ATMOSPHERE:
- Vibrant, active urban atmosphere with appropriate energy level
- Sky reflects the city environment — may include reflections in glazed facades
- Active street life creates cinematic urban storytelling
